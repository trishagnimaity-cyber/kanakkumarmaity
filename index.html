The Artist

Kanak Kumar Maity

A sculptor with over three decades of experience, Kanak Kumar Maity has dedicated his life to the ancient and soulful art of form-making. Trained in Dokra casting — a 4,000-year-old lost-wax tradition — he creates works that breathe with cultural memory and artistic intention.


Holding a B.F.A. (1994) and M.F.A. (1996), he has exhibited across India's most prestigious galleries and worked as an educator — most notably at Tihar Central Jail, New Delhi, using art as a tool of transformation.


His work spans ceramics, sculpture, and mixed media — each piece a dialogue between clay, metal, and the human spirit.
